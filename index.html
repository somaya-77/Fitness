instructions

sports tips :

Who among us does not dream of a slim body, good health, and an easier life, but it requires studying a lot of information about nutrition and fitness training.

The perfect time to exercise :

The truth is that there is no perfect time, and it is up to the desire and ability of the trainee, some prefer to exercise in the early morning, and others prefer to exercise at night, but as a general rule it is preferable to exercise after two or three hours after eating so as not to cause nausea.

Snack before exercise :

There are some athletes who eat a light meal that provides energy before starting the exercise, such as a fruit, and it is preferable to avoid eating meals rich in sugar and soft drinks, and it is also possible to carry some starchy foods to maintain the rate of energy during exercise, such as brown bread or bananas.

The need to warm up before exercising :

The warm-up is necessary to alert the muscles for the upcoming exercises, and the warm-up exercises differ from normal exercises, as they are with slow and light movements such as light walking with a gradient of walking speed until you reach light jogging, and the ideal time to warm up is eight minutes, and the warm-up process works to send oxygen to the muscles, and ensures the work of the body with better efficiency.

Aerobic exercises are important for the large muscles in the body :

It is a set of exercises that target moving the large muscles of the body in rhythm for a continuous period of time, which is important for improving the health of the heart and lungs, burning calories, and controlling weight such as jogging, walking, cycling, and swimming.

Strength exercises to build muscle :

They are weightlifting exercises of different weights, and they burn calories, build and strengthen muscles, increase bone density and strengthen joints, making them increase a person's ability to perform daily tasks without getting tired, as the increase in muscle mass is directly proportional to the increase in burning calories.

The importance of stretching in exercise :

Stretching improves the flexibility and balance of the muscles, so the trainee must be careful to do the stretching exercises in a correct and safe manner. During the stretching, it is preferable to breathe deeply and regularly, and the appropriate time to stretch is after the end of the exercises for five minutes.

Make time to rest your muscles :

After each exercise, it is preferable to allocate time to rest the muscles and regulate breathing and heart rate. It also strengthens the muscles from the occurrence of stiffness and acute pain.

Pay attention to drinking water during exercise :

It is important to drink fluids during exercises that last for more than half an hour, and for exercises that exceed this time, it is preferable to drink sports drinks that help energy levels, especially drinks that contain sodium, to compensate for the loss of a large amount of sodium through sweating.
